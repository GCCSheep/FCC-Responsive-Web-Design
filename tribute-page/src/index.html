Ayrton Senna

Racing driver and three-time Formula One world champion

Time line of Ayrton Senna's life:

  • 1960 - Born in SÃ£o Paulo, Brazil.
  • 1973 - Begins his motorsport career in karting.
  • 1981 - Moves up to open-wheel racing.
  • 1983 - Wins the British Formula Three Championship.
  • 1984 - Makes his Formula One debut with Toleman-Hart.
  • 1985 - Moves to Lotus-Renault the following year and wins six Grands Prix over the next three seasons.
  • 1988 - Joins Frenchman Alain Prost at McLaren-Honda. Between them, they win all but one of the 16 Grands Prix that year, and Senna claims his first World Championship.
  • 1990 - Claims his second championship.
  • 1991 - Claims his third championship.
  • 1993 - Manages to finish the season as runner-up, winning five races.
  • 1994 - Negotiates a move to Williams. Later that year, dies in an accident leading the San Marino Grand Prix.
